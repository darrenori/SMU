# Privacy notice

IKEBOT 2.0 processes all inputs within the browser.

The prototype:

- does not call an external model or API;
- does not request Chrome permissions;
- does not include analytics, advertising or telemetry;
- does not transmit conversations or matter details;
- stores the current matter, checklist state and conversation history in local browser storage; and
- lets the user clear local history or delete the local matter at any time.

Transcript exports are created locally and downloaded through the browser. Once exported, the file is governed by the user's own storage and handling controls.

The privacy scanner detects only a small set of common patterns, such as email addresses, phone numbers and Singapore NRIC/FIN-like identifiers. It cannot identify every form of confidential, privileged or personal information. Lawyer review remains essential.
