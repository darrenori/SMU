# Workflow catalogue

IKEBOT includes nine preparation frameworks:

1. Client intake
2. Singapore legal research plan
3. Plain-language clause explanation
4. Deadline triage
5. Matter chronology
6. Case brief
7. Document review
8. Client correspondence
9. Hearing preparation

Each workflow contains five review headings with a concise prompt. Matching is deterministic: the engine scores a small, visible keyword list and returns the highest-scoring framework. Users may bypass matching with slash commands such as `/intake`, `/research` and `/deadline`.

The workflows organise work; they do not determine facts, retrieve law or provide advice.
