# Content guidelines

IKEBOT uses concise, procedural language suitable for professional legal work.

- Describe the action a lawyer should take.
- Separate factual collection, legal verification and professional judgment.
- Prefer “check”, “record”, “confirm” and “verify” over unsupported conclusions.
- Do not imply that an indicative date is a legal deadline.
- Link official sources as starting points without describing them as exhaustive.
- Do not fabricate citations, cases, statutes or quotations.
- Keep disclaimers close to higher-risk tools without overwhelming ordinary use.
