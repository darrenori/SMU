A better starting point

Less searching.
More lawyering.

IKEBOT was designed around the small, repeatable tasks that interrupt focused legal work.

01

Structure the first conversation

Turn an initial client question into a practical intake checklist and a set of follow-up prompts.

02

Frame the research path

Break a broad issue into research questions and identify the authoritative Singapore sources to check.

03

Explain the complex clearly

Create a plain-language first pass that lawyers can review, refine and use in client communications.

04

Plan dates carefully

Calculate indicative calendar or weekday dates while keeping legal verification steps visible.

05

Protect matter information

Scan common identifiers, redact them locally and work through a five-point review checklist.

06

Start with primary sources

Search a curated library of official Singapore legal resources without sending a query anywhere.

Private by architecture

Your matter stays
in your browser.

01

You enter a task

IKEBOT normalises the text and checks for common personal identifiers on the device.

02

A workflow is matched

Transparent keyword rules select a fixed preparation framework. There is no probabilistic output.

03

You verify the result

The lawyer remains responsible for authorities, calculations, advice and the final work product.
