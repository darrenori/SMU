A better starting point

Less searching.
More lawyering.

IKEBOT was designed around the small, repeatable tasks that interrupt focused legal work.

01

Structure the first conversation

Turn an initial client question into a practical intake checklist and a set of follow-up prompts.

02

Frame the research path

Break a broad issue into research questions and identify the authoritative Singapore sources to check.

03

Explain the complex clearly

Create a plain-language first pass that lawyers can review, refine and use in client communications.
